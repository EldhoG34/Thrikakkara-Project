WELCOME TO THE

TOWN OF THRIKKAKARA
We’re a friendly and vibrant community in Monroe County, NY with a population of over 20,000 residents.

Located just 15 minutes from downtown Rochester, our Town is conveniently located near many of the region’s attractions, while still offering the benefits of rural living. Centered in the Town is the quaint Village of Spencerport on the Erie Canal.

Our website contains information for residents, businesses, and visitors to the Town. Explore our pages to learn more about services, departments, and events in the Town of Ogden. The Ogden Community Center houses the Ogden Police Department, Ogden Farmers’ Library, our Parks and Recreation facility and the rest of the Town’s government offices. Our staff are welcoming and approachable, and always eager to help residents in any way we can.

The Town of Ogden is a safe and growing community filled with close-knit families and longtime residents.

For more information and/or assistance, please contact Ogden Town Supervisor, Mike Zale at 617-6100 or reach him by email at supervisor@ogdenny.com
